case study


OCEARCH shark tracker
Website and app where you can track ocean animals.
Ocearch is a global non-profit organization that conducts unprecedented research on the ocean wildlife, specifically sharks, in order to help scientists collect data.

Most effective
Use of technology
Implications
The interactive map
Shows all the animals' locations and where they have been. Additionally, they all have adorable names.
The shark tracker
By putting trackers on sharks and other animals, researchers can track their locations and swim patterns, offering more data and evidence. This helps scientists gain more data and information about ocean animals that do not have much as of right now.
Create a tracker or counter of some sort, but for ocean trash
Mark locations of where disregarded fishing nets and other pollutants are on a map. Hope to gain more knowledge over the ocean pollution and the wildlife in the sea.
