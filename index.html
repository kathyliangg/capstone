Fishing Gear and the Hazardous Effects on Marine Life

    Ghost Fishing
  • FIshing gear lost or abandoned out at sea can trap fish, crustaceans, birds, and other animals that are not meant to be caught
  • It can be especially harmful to vulnerable populations, such as endangered and protected species, including turtles and marine mammals

    Causes Ocean Pollution
  • Abandoned, lost, and discarded fishing gear, also known as ALDFG Is a major contributor on ocean pollution
  • 75% to 86% of all plastic waste in the GPGP identified as coming from offshore fishing

    What Species can be Hurt?
  • In 2011, most victims of improperly discarded fishing gear were Canada Geese (25%)
  • Other animals who were affected were sea turtles, bald eagles, owls, and herons
  • Of the fishing gear victims admitted to the Wildlife Center since 2011, nearly 60 percent of the patients died or were euthanized as a result of their injuries
